### Vegetables for the Goldfish
Below are the list of fruits and vegetables the Gold fish feeds on.
1. Peas - helps at digestion
2. Lettuce
3. Spinach
4. Zucchini
5. Carrots
6. Cucumbers
7. Apples
8. Grapes
9. Garlic
10. Pieces of Orange (for vitamin C)
11. Rice
12. Broccoli
13. Corn
14. Beans

Remember to cook and chop all hard vegetables
